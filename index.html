What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a number that shows whether your weight is healthy for your height. It is calculated using a person’s weight (kg) divided by height (m)².

Why is BMI Important?

BMI helps you understand your health category:

  • Underweight
  • Normal weight
  • Overweight
  • Obesity

Limitations

BMI does not measure body fat directly and may not be accurate for:

  • Muscular people
  • Children
  • Elderly
  • Pregnant women
